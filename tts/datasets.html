Datasets¶

LJSpeech¶

LJSpeech is a speech dataset that consists of a single female, English speaker. It contains approximately 24 hours of speech.

Obtaining and prepocessing the data for NeMo can be done with our helper script:

python scripts/get_ljspeech_data.py --data_root=<where_you_want_to_save_data>
